Hospitality Foundation

Front of House, TONO

Dec 2025 – Present

Before and alongside product design, I spent 10 years across hospitality teams as a barista, FOH supervisor, server, runner, busser, and host in the US, Canada, and Australia — including Westin, W Hotels, Marriott, and Harbour Rocks Hotel. That experience taught me how operations really break: under pressure, across handoffs, and inside tools people do not have time to fight with.

TonoTip came from that reality. I designed and built the tip distribution tool as a side project while working front-of-house at TONO. It was later adopted by the team, replacing manual calculations with a shared workflow used in real service conditions.
