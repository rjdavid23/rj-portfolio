WiredIn

Event planning platform for team coordination

Centralized event planning across tasks, timelines, and stakeholders. Reduced coordination overhead and improved visibility.

Product Designer
Capstone Project
End-to-end design

Overview

WiredIn is a capstone project focused on improving how teams plan and coordinate events. Existing workflows relied heavily on fragmented tools, manual tracking, and constant back-and-forth communication.

Problem

  • Fragmented tools across planning stages
  • Lack of visibility into task ownership
  • Repetitive communication and follow-ups
  • Difficulty tracking progress and deadlines

Solution

Designed a centralized platform that:

  • Organizes tasks and responsibilities clearly
  • Improves team coordination and communication
  • Reduces reliance on scattered tools
  • Simplifies planning workflows end-to-end
