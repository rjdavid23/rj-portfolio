# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Hiring managers evaluating design craft for roles; design peers following the work; clients and founders considering freelance or contract engagements. All arrive expecting to quickly assess skill level, design sensibility, and real-world impact.

## Product Purpose

RJ David's portfolio — a curated showcase of product design work spanning internal tools (TonoTip), B2B SaaS (Deliverect), and capstone projects (WiredIn). Success means a visitor leaves with a clear, confident understanding of RJ's design thinking, craft quality, and ability to ship real products.

## Brand Personality

Craft, thoughtful, understated. Quiet confidence — the work speaks for itself. Warm but not cozy. Minimal but not cold. Human and approachable, never corporate or stiff.

## Anti-references

Corporate/stiff aesthetics. Generic startup/SaaS landing page patterns. Overdesigned or artsy portfolios where form obscures function. Anything that feels templated or mass-produced.

## Design Principles

1. **The work is the hero** — Every layout decision serves the case studies. Decoration never competes with content.
2. **Quiet confidence** — No loud UI, no gratuitous effects. Polish shows in spacing, alignment, typography, and restraint.
3. **Clear before clever** — Navigation, hierarchy, and copy are immediately understandable. Users find what they need without thinking.
4. **Human warmth** — Copy and interactions feel personal, not corporate. The portfolio reflects a real person who makes things.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AAA target (7:1 contrast ratio for text). No reliance on color alone for conveying information. Keyboard navigable. Reduced motion supported.
